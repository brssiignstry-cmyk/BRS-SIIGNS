6. Factors Affecting the Final Price in 2026

Several things decide the exact amount you will pay:

  • Size: Bigger signs need more materials and stronger power systems.
  • Design Complexity: Simple text is cheaper. Adding your logo or custom shapes increases the price.
  • Brightness Level: We use different LED modules depending on how bright you want the sign to be even in direct sunlight, a technology discussed in LEDs Magazine.
  • Installation Height: Ground floor is easier. First-floor or higher installations need extra safety equipment.
This is why comparing neon vs flex requires looking at the total engineering package.
