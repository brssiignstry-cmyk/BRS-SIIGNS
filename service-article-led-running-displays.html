2. What exactly is a LED Running Display?

A LED running display Trichy (also known as a scrolling board or digital ticker) is a weather-sealed aluminium cabinet containing high-brightness LED modules. These modules are controlled by a specialized microcontroller chip that can store and display thousands of characters, graphics, and even simple animations. They are readable in direct afternoon sun and provide incredible visibility at night without the need for external floodlights. At BRS SIIGNS, we offer sizes ranging from 4x3 ft entrance boards to massive 12x8 ft shopfront displays. Understanding how LED sign boards can help is the first step to digital transformation.
