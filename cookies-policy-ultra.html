2. Technical Overview of Cookies

Cookies are essentially tiny text files that a web browser stores on your device. They act as a memory for the website, allowing it to recognize your device when you return. This technology is a standard part of internet privacy and is widely discussed on platforms like Reddit r/webdev. For us, they help remember your interest in 3D metal letters or acrylic edge lit signs.
