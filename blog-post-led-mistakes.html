12. 15-Point Buying FAQ Masterclass

It usually means low-grade chips and poor weatherproofing, leading to flickering and fading within months.

3. How can I tell if the acrylic is UV-stabilized? +
Ask for the brand name (like Mitsubishi or Astariglas) and a written guarantee against yellowing for 5+ years.
4. Is IP65 really necessary for Trichy? +
Yes, it's essential to survive the heavy monsoon rains and high humidity without short-circuiting.
