Who We Are

Founded in the heart of Tiruchirappalli, BRS SIIGNS has evolved from a local workshop into a state-wide leader in signage engineering. Our journey began with a simple realization: most signage in India fails within 2-3 years due to poor electrical design and material selection. We decided to change that.

As an engineer-owned company, we approach every project with a focus on durability, electrical safety, and brand impact. Whether it's a massive 3D LED pylon for a corporate headquarters or delicate acrylic mementos for an awards ceremony, our commitment to quality remains unwavering. We don't just build signs; we engineer trust.

We take pride in our "Zero Subcontracting" policy. Every piece of signage that carries the BRS name is designed, fabricated, and tested in our own facility in Trichy. This allows us to maintain strict quality control and offer transparent, factory-direct pricing to our clients.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What types of signs do you specialize in? +
We specialize in LED Neon Signs, 3D Metal Letters (SS304), Acrylic Edge Lit Boards, LED Running Displays, and high-durability Vinyl Flex Banners.
2. How long does a typical installation take? +
From design approval to final glow, it typically takes 5-7 working days depending on the complexity of the project.
3. Do you provide design services? +
Yes! We provide a Free 3D Design Proof within 24 hours for every project before we start fabrication.
4. What is the warranty on your LED signs? +
We provide a 1-year comprehensive warranty on LEDs and drivers, and a 5-year guarantee on the structural integrity of the materials.
5. Are your signs energy-efficient? +
Absolutely. Our engineered LED designs consume up to 60% less power than standard market alternatives.
6. Do you handle the electrical installation? +
Yes, our team handles the complete installation, including the electrical wiring and surge protection setup.
7. Can you repair signs made by other companies? +
While we prioritize our own installations, we do offer professional auditing and repair services for existing signage systems.
8. What materials do you use for 3D letters? +
We use premium SS304 grade stainless steel, high-grade acrylic, and UV-stable coatings to ensure zero fading and zero rust.
9. Do you serve areas outside of Trichy? +
Yes, we serve all 32 districts of Tamil Nadu, including Chennai, Madurai, Coimbatore, and Salem.
10. How do I get a quote? +
You can click the WhatsApp button on our site or call us at 094867 50872 for a free engineering consultation and quote.
11. What is the difference between Neon and LED Neon? +
Traditional neon uses fragile glass tubes and high voltage. LED Neon Flex uses flexible silicone tubes and low voltage, making it safer and more durable.
12. Do you provide maintenance services? +
Yes, we offer annual maintenance contracts (AMC) to keep your signs clean and functioning at peak efficiency.
13. Are your banners weather-proof? +
Our Vinyl & Flex banners feature a unique UV-shield coating that protects them from heavy rain and extreme sun exposure.
14. Can you create custom mementos for corporate events? +
Yes, we design and fabricate premium laser-engraved trophies and plaques for corporate, school, and temple events.
15. Why choose an engineer-owned company? +
Because we prioritize electrical safety, structural integrity, and long-term performance over just "looking good" for a few months.
