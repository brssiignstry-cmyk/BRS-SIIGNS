12. 15-Point Acrylic Maintenance FAQ Masterclass

1. Can I use glass cleaner on my acrylic sign? +
No, glass cleaners often contain ammonia or alcohol which can cause "crazing" or tiny cracks in the acrylic.
2. What is the best way to remove dust? +
Use a dry microfiber cloth. For stubborn dust, use a blower or a slightly damp microfiber cloth (water only).
3. How often should I clean my sign? +
In a typical Trichy office, a light dusting once a week and a deep clean every 6 months is recommended.
4. Can I buff out a scratch? +
Minor surface scratches can often be buffed out with specialized acrylic polishing compounds.
5. Does the sign need to be turned off for cleaning? +
Yes, always power off and unplug the sign before cleaning to avoid any electrical risks.
6. What happens if the LEDs start to flicker? +
Flickering is usually a sign of a failing power adapter or a loose connection. Check the plug and adapter first.
7. Is it safe to leave the sign on 24/7? +
Yes, our signs are designed for continuous use, but turning them off at night will extend the life of the LEDs.
8. Can humidity damage the sign? +
High humidity can cause condensation inside the frame. Our signs are perimeter-sealed to minimize this risk.
9. Does direct sunlight affect the acrylic? +
We use only UV-stable PMMA, but prolonged direct sunlight can still cause some fading over many years.
10. How do I clean the engraved parts? +
Use a soft-bristled brush or a blower to remove dust from the engraved grooves.
11. Can I use a vacuum cleaner? +
Only if it has a soft brush attachment. Be careful not to let the hard plastic nozzle touch the acrylic.
