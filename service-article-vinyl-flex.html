2. What exactly is "Shield" Protection?

Shield is a clear, scratch-resistant protective laminate applied over the printed surface. Think of it as "sunscreen" for your banner. UV radiation, rain, and dust hit the Shield layer—not the ink underneath. Combined with our high-quality UV-resistant inks, this creates a barrier that significantly extends the life of your branding. At BRS SIIGNS, we’ve completed over 400 Shield installations, proving that weather-proof vinyl flex is the only logical choice for long-term outdoor use. 10. 5 Tips for Banner Longevity

  1. Proper Tensioning: A loose banner flaps and tears. Tension it like a drum.
  2. Avoid Harsh Chemicals: Clean only with mild soap and water.
  3. Wind Vents: For large banners in windy areas, we cut "wind slits" to reduce pressure.
  4. Edge Reinforcement: Always opt for folded and stitched hems.
  5. UV Shield: Never skip the lamination for south-facing signs.
