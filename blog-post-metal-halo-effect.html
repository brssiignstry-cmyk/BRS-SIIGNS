1. What is Halo-Lighting? The Science of Indirect Glow

Halo-lighting (or reverse channel lighting) is where the LEDs are mounted inside the metal letter, facing the wall. This is a psychological shortcut discussed in Wired's features on visual attention. The light reflects off the wall, creating a soft "halo" around the letter. For 3D Metal Letters Trichy, this creates a "divine" and sophisticated look.
