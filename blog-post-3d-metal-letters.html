12. 15-Point Metal Signage FAQ Masterclass

1. Why is SS304 better than SS202 for Trichy? +
SS304 has higher nickel content, making it much more resistant to corrosion in Trichy's humid and industrial environment compared to SS202.
2. Can metal letters be illuminated? +
Yes, we typically use "Halo Lighting" (back-lit) or front-lit acrylic faces with metal sides (returns).
3. What is Titanium Gold coating? +
It's a PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) coating that molecularly bonds a gold-colored layer to the steel, ensuring it never peels or fades.
4. How long does a metal sign last? +
A high-quality SS304 sign is designed to last 10–15 years with minimal maintenance.
5. Are metal signs heavier to install? +
Yes, they require specialized mounting brackets and structural support, which BRS SIIGNS provides as part of the installation.
6. Can you match my brand's specific font? +
Yes, our precision laser cutting captures every detail of your brand's typography and logo.
7. What is a "brushed" finish? +

Yes, they are very popular for corporate reception areas and luxury hotel lobbies.
